Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Fit to Practise ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. Your information will never be shared with any third party for marketing purposes.

Information we collect

  • Details you give us — your name, email address, and any information you choose to share when you register, contact us, or work with a mentor.
  • Learning activity — the courses you enrol in and your progress, so we can show you your dashboard.
  • Technical data — basic, anonymised information about how the site is used, to help us improve it.

How we use your information

  • To provide your courses, mentorship, and learning dashboard.
  • To respond to your enquiries and provide support.
  • To send you updates you have asked to receive (you can opt out at any time).
  • To keep the service secure and improve it over time.

Confidentiality

Everything you share with your mentor is treated in the strictest confidence. We understand the sensitivity of the Fitness to Practise process and handle your information with care.

Cookies & local storage

We use your browser's local storage to remember your cart, enrolments, and progress so the site works as you move between pages. We do not use advertising or cross-site tracking cookies.

Your rights

Under UK data protection law you have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data, and to object to certain processing. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@fit2practise.com.

Contact us

If you have any questions about this policy or how we handle your data, email hello@fit2practise.com and we'll be happy to help.

This policy is a template provided for the website and should be reviewed by a qualified professional before you rely on it.
