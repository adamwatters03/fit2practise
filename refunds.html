Refund & Returns

Last updated: July 2026

We want you to be confident in your purchase. If a course isn't right for you, we offer a straightforward 14-day refund policy.

Our 14-day guarantee

If you're not satisfied with a course, you can request a full refund within 14 days of your purchase — no complicated hoops to jump through.

How to request a refund

  • Email hello@fit2practise.com within 14 days of your purchase.
  • Include the email address you used and the course name.
  • We'll confirm and process your refund to your original payment method, usually within a few working days.

Exceptions

Refunds may not apply where a course has been substantially completed or a certificate has been issued, or for 1:1 mentorship sessions that have already taken place. If you're unsure, just ask — we'll always try to help.

Questions?

If anything is unclear, contact us at hello@fit2practise.com and we'll be happy to talk it through.

This policy is a template provided for the website and should be reviewed by a qualified professional before you rely on it.
